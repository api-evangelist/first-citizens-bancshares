# First Citizens BancShares

> First Citizens BancShares is a super-regional financial holding company (parent of First-Citizens Bank & Trust). Its programmable surface runs through Silicon Valley Bank (SVB) — acquired in 2023 and now a division of First Citizens — whose Apigee-backed developer portal at developer.svb.com publishes a family of partner-gated commercial banking REST APIs for authorization, balances, transfers, ACH, instant payments, wires, stop payment, virtual cards, webhooks, and reference/reachability. Access is partner-gated (commercial onboarding), secured with OAuth 2.0 client-credentials plus detached JWS request signing.

## APIs

- [SVB Authorization API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/authorization-api/1.0/authorization): OAuth2 client-credentials token issuance + JWS request-signing model.
- [SVB Account Balance API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/account-balance/1.0/account-balance-overview): Current and available balances for commercial deposit accounts.
- [SVB Account Transfer API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/account-transfer/1.0/account-transfer-overview): Book transfers between a client's own SVB accounts.
- [SVB ACH Transfers API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/ach-transfers/2.1/ach-overview): Domestic ACH credits/debits, verified ACH, and IAT domestic entries (v2.1).
- [SVB Instant Payments API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/instant-payments/1.0/instant-payments-overview): Real-time, irrevocable payments over U.S. instant rails.
- [SVB Wires API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/wires-api/2.1/wires-overview): Domestic and international wire initiation and management (v2.1).
- [SVB Stop Payment API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/stop-payment/1.0/stop-payment-overview): Place and inspect stop-payment orders on checks.
- [SVB Virtual Cards API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/virtual-cards/2.0/virtual-cards-vcn-overview): Issue and manage virtual card numbers for AP/procurement/travel (v2.0).
- [SVB Webhook API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/webhook-api/2.0/webhooks-overview): Asynchronous event notifications via subscriptions under /v2/event/* (v2.0).
- [SVB Reference API](https://developer.svb.com/apis/commercial-banking-apis/reference-api/1.0/reference-overview): Reachability lookups (e.g. instant-payments creditor-agent reachability).

## Auth

- OAuth 2.0 client-credentials: POST client_id/client_secret to https://api.svb.com/v1/security/oauth/token for a bearer token.
- Detached JWS request signing (RFC 7515) via the x-jws-signature header on each request body.
- Access is partner-gated: onboarding through SVB / First Citizens commercial relationship.
